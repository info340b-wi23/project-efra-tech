App Description

The main users for this application are family members. This app intends to strengthen the connection and communication between family members in today's digitalized age, by bringing their digitalized interactions on this app back into a closely-functioning household. We are envisioning an average American family where people tend to get busy enough that kids forget to do their chores, or keep up with family time and events, so this platform intends to hold information that help makes every family member's life a little easier, so to say.

That being said, this app intends to minimize the amount "reminding" that parents need to facilitate in real life, by putting it all in one app instead.
